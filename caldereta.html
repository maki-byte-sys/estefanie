Caldereta
Ingredients

  • 500g beef stew meat (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup liver spread or pÃ¢tÃ©
  • 1 cup potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 cup carrots (sliced)
  • Olives (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

How to Cook
Caldereta

  1. Heat oil and sautÃ© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add beef cubes and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 30 minutes until beef is tender.
  4. Add liver spread and mix well.
  5. Add potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Add olives (optional) and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
