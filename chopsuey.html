Chop Suey
Ingredients

  • 300g pork or chicken (sliced thin)
  • 1 cup carrots (sliced)
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1 cup cabbage (sliced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

How to Cook
Chop Suey

  1. Heat a pan and sautÃ© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced pork or chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add carrots, green beans, and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add cabbage and pour in chicken broth.
  5. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. If desired, mix cornstarch with a little water and add to thicken the sauce.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
