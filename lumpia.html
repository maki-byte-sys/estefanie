Lumpia
Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 cup carrots (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup cabbage (chopped)
  • 1 onion (minced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil

How to Cook
Lumpia

  1. Combine pork, vegetables, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place mixture on wrapper and roll tightly.
  3. Seal edges with water.
  4. Heat oil in a pan.
  5. Fry until golden brown.
  6. Drain and serve hot.

Source: Kawaling Pinoy
