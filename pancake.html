Pancake
Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter or oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

How to Cook
Pancake

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve with syrup, fruits, or toppings of your choice.
