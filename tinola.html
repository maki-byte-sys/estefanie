Tinola
Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 green papaya (sliced) or chayote
  • 1 cup chili leaves or malunggay leaves
  • 4 cups water or chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

How to Cook
Tinola

  1. Heat oil in a pot. SautÃ© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add fish sauce and stir to combine.
  4. Pour water or chicken broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for 20-25 minutes until chicken is tender.
  5. Add green papaya or chayote and cook until tender.
  6. Add chili leaves or malunggay leaves. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Source: Kawaling Pinoy
